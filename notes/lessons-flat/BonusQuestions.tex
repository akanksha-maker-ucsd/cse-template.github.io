    \begin{itemize}
    \item[] Basis Step: $\A\A \in X$, $\C\C \in X$, $\U\U \in X$, $\G\G \in X$
    \item[] Recursive Step: If $x \in X$ and $b \in B$ then $bxb \in X$
    \end{itemize}

    Give three example elements of $X$ and an English description of the set.
